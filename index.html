Interactive USGS Funding Map and Bar Chart

By Nick Kolarik and Jeremiah Lant

Description

Interactive map and chart of overhead for USGS Water Science Centers by state during the year 2018. The data is provided by the United States Geological Survey (USGS). The choropleth map and bar chart show total overhead percentages by state according to the associated Water Science Center (WSC).
